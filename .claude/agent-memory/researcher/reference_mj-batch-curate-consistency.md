The "batch and curate" reality for consistent MJ style at scale is BROADLY ATTESTED across guides + community, but framed two ways that must be kept distinct.

**Thesis A — curation IS the consistency/quality mechanism (selection-after-volume):**
- VVSVS Log 003 (Ivan, practitioner w/ 5.6M-view viral hit): "You are the curator. You protect your audience from the flood." / "Their work doesn't feel like AI because they only show the 1% that transcends the medium." / "One great post is worth 100 sloppy ones." — https://vvsvs.pro/logs/log-003-engineering-attention-with-the-kinetic-workflow
- Jim Gulsen (Medium): "tasking an army of junior designers to keep iterating solutions creatively, then handpicking a small percentage of the results for publishing" + "generate hundreds of images to choose from in minutes" — https://medium.com/@jgulsen_97727/using-undraw-and-midjourney-for-illustration-a-step-by-step-guide-89055751b5ca
- cardesignnews (Ideation as curation): "Curation is one of the most critical strengths for a designer in our time" / "Tweaking the prompt could give you 20 or 30 in less than ten minutes. An hour's work could give you too many to choose from." — https://www.cardesignnews.com/design-tools/ideation-with-midjourney-an-exercise-in-curation/304910

**Hit-rate language (community lore, not official):**
- runtheprompts: "If one prompt produces four options, chances are 20% of them will be pure magic, per the classic 80/20 rule." — https://runtheprompts.com/resources/midjourney-info/11-easy-midjourney-prompting-tips-techniques/
- dirtcastle (Threads, anecdote): "The Midjourney feed might be 99.9% garbage, but there are still gems to be found!" — https://www.threads.com/@dirtcastle/post/DEvnvoTSpzb

**Batch+select feed workflow (IG):**
- Prompt Palette 4-week loop: W1 "Generate 50 images... Don't post yet just experiment." → W2 "Select your top 10. Post them and track engagement." → W3 "Double down on the style that performed best. Create 20 variations." → W4 "Build your signature aesthetic." — https://medium.com/@PromptPaletteAI/master-midjourney-7-prompts-...-0b1fae4acd78

**Thesis B — COUNTERPOINT: some pros say consistency comes from INPUTS, not selection.** Jamey Gannon (AI creative director, on Lenny's Newsletter + ChatPRD "How I AI"): consistency via moodboards + sref + personalization codes + strategic iteration — "a manicured process of visual communication with the AI," NOT generate-many-pick-winners. So curation-as-consistency is the dominant but NOT universal view. — https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/p/mastering-midjourney-how-to-create

How to apply: when advising the project on style-at-scale, both levers are real and complementary — tighten inputs (sref/moodboard/raw+low-s recipe) to RAISE hit-rate, AND accept curation as the final consistency gate. Don't promise every gen on-style. Style feedback loop (feed best output back as sref) is supported by Style Creator refinement rounds (5-10 to stabilize) but I found no strong pro testimonial explicitly describing "my best output -> new sref" as a named loop — flag as thin.
