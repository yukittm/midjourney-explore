# Idea Bank — conceptual prompts in the reference artists' vein

Subject ideas for the blend moodboard (`blend-v1`). The reference artists (`giz.akdag`,
`marianopeccinetti`) get their strength from **an ordinary subject + one surreal conceptual twist**,
rendered with clean editorial/filmic treatment. The *idea* carries the artistry and the moodboard
carries the *style* — so spend prompt words on the **subject/scene**, specified richly within the
~40-word budget, not on style. (The seed prompts in the table below are terse concept sketches; flesh
them to production detail when generating — see [[prompting-guide]].)

## The conceptual moves (the "recipe")

1. **Scale inversion** — giant / tiny (a man riding a giant antelope; a person on a tower in a favela).
2. **Impossible juxtaposition** — right subject, wrong context (horses among clouds; people on Saturn's ring).
3. **Material swap** — substance becomes something else (cotton-candy-cloud legs; a liquid-marble figure).
4. **Mundane act on a cosmic stage** — an everyday action staged in a vast / poetic place (playing soccer on an endless plain).
5. **Object as monument** — an everyday object made huge / central in a landscape.

> Generative method: pick a subject the user loves (e.g. **soccer / sports / dynamic figures**) ×
> one move above → a strong idea.
