## 0. The one root cause of growth
**A recognizable, repeatable signature that survives the swipe.** Every large AI-art account has one;
it is the strongest common factor in their growth 🟡. Our **"flowing color wave" editorial look IS this lever** —
so the prime directive is **protect the signature**: keep the main grid coherent, don't post
off-signature experiments to it. (giz.akdag = treatment-signature path; Tropland = named-world path —
we are the former.) [research: AI-art playbook]

## 1. The 2025-26 reality (what actually changed)
- **Instagram is a search / discovery engine, not a hashtag feed.** ✅ Hashtags do **not** boost reach
  (Mosseri, Feb 2025); posts capped at **5 hashtags** (Dec 18 2025). Public professional posts are
  **indexed by Google** since Jul 2025 (professional accounts; opt-out in Settings).
- **Reels = the cold-reach engine** (reaches non-followers). Carousels & stills mostly serve people who
  already follow you and earn **saves**. ✅ (per-surface ranking; Buffer 4M-post data: video > carousel > single).
- **Signal hierarchy: watch-time → sends (DM-shares) → saves → likes.** Sends & saves *compound*
  (a DM-share reads as an active recommendation; a save = "worth returning to"). 🟡 exact weight ratios are analyst estimates.

## 2. Positioning (fill in the blanks)
- **Handle:** `@timbankrupt` (Tim Bankrupt) · **Name field (SEO):** **`Digital · AI Art`** ✅ (high-weight in IG search).
  **Trade-off:** leading with "AI / Digital art" optimizes for the *prompt-curious / creators*, not *art buyers* — fine while growth-first; revisit for a collector/print path.
- **One-liner:** flowing-color, geometric editorial art — *"where color becomes motion."*
- **Self-label AI honestly** via the in-app toggle. ✅ Meta says the label is for transparency and does
  **not** itself cut reach; severe penalties attach to **deepfakes/face-body manipulation**, not stylized
  generative art. (Blog claims of "15-80% reach loss" 🟡 contradict Meta's own statement — treat as unverified.)

## 3. Content engine (the three formats + Trial Reels)
| Format | Job | How (for this account) |
|---|---|---|
| **Reels** ✅ | **Cold reach / new followers** | Process → iteration → **reveal**; before/after; satisfying transitions across ONE color theme. ~15-30s. Design the first ~1.7s for watch-time (open on the most striking frame / the transformation). Match cover frame to first frame (🟡 +saves). Make hero frames **DM-shareable**. |
| **Trial Reels** ✅ | **Risk-free experiments** | Shown to **non-followers only**, **not** on your grid/Reels tab; after ~24h you see the numbers. **Use it to test new subjects / color-chords on cold audiences without diluting the curated grid** — directly serves "converge on a consistent style." |
| **Carousels** ✅ | **Saves + depth** | Keep it tight (~6-10; IG allows up to 20 but long sets lose completion 🟡); slide 1 = hook. "How I made this" / palette breakdown / a numbered **series**. Inherently save-worthy. |
| **Stills** | **Grid identity** | Curated hero pieces. **Pin the 3 strongest** — visitors judge the style fast 🟡. Signature-art profile photo (not a face/logo). |

**Texture caveat** 🟡: 2026 visual grammar rewards *controlled imperfection* (grain, film emulation) and
penalizes AI-slickness. Our `--style raw` + low `--s` kernel already pushes this — keep it; a too-clean
render risks reading as generic AI.

## 3b. Phase 0 — cold start (first ~30 days, zero audience)
- **Build a 9-12 piece signature "runway" BEFORE going public** — the first profile visitor must see a
  coherent grid that defines the style, not 3 posts in a ghost town. The single biggest 0-follower lever.
- **Reels-first + Trial Reels only.** At zero followers, Reels are the *only* cold-reach surface;
  carousels & Stories reach ~nobody yet. First ~4 public posts = process/reveal Reels; **hold
  carousels/Stories until there's a base.** (§4's mix is steady-state, not cold-start.)
- **Pin the 3 strongest** the moment you launch.

## 4. Cadence
**3-5 posts/week, sustainable; consistency > raw frequency.** Suggested mix ≈ 3-4 Reels + 2-3 carousels +
1-2 stills/week + daily Stories. **Space posts out** (spacing performs better than dumping several at once). 🟡
"Daily Reels" is aspirational — **never sacrifice the per-image quality that is our whole differentiator** to hit a count.

## 5. Discoverability (this replaces hashtag-stuffing)
- **Keyword SEO** ✅ — IG indexes username, **Name field**, bio (**first ~80 chars weighted**), captions
  (write a real first sentence with natural keywords), and **alt text** (a descriptive sentence per post —
  also feeds Google since Jul 2025; describe, don't keyword-stuff).
- **Hashtags:** **3-5 niche** tags max, caption **or** first comment (both work the same now). Niche
  descriptors over mega-tags (`#art` = invisible). A weak topical hint, not a reach driver. ✅ cap = 5.

## 6. Engagement & growth amplifiers
- **Reply fast to early comments** — early replies tend to seed a comment thread. 🟡
- **Save/Send CTAs** — specific ("save this for a grey day," "send to someone who feels in color"), never
  crude engagement-bait ("like if…" is penalized ✅).
- **Collab posts** with adjacent AI artists — a Collab appears in **both** accounts' feeds (up to 4
  collaborators); cross-pollinates audiences. giz grew partly by **style-blend collabs**. 🟡 exact lift figures.
- **Give the craft away** (process, palette/sref talk) — converts viewers → followers and seeds any future product.
- **Broadcast channel** — later, once there's a core audience worth broadcasting to.

## 7. Monetization (LATER — audience first, every source agrees)
1. **sref / preset pack** (giz model — a treatment-signature account's best fit; she prices a 300+ bundle at **~$200**).
2. **Prints / print-on-demand** (Mariano, van Dieken) — editorial color suits wall art.
3. **Brand / licensing** — highest ceiling, slowest; flows from visibility + a portfolio that reads "art-direction-ready."
4. Courses/prompt books — later. **Ambassadorships/features are byproducts of being recognizable, not a plan.**

## 8. Pitfalls (ranked)
1. **Style drift** — the #1 growth killer 🟡. Off-signature → Trial Reels, not the grid.
2. **Over-glossy "AI look"** — bake in texture (raw kernel).
3. **Selling before an audience exists** — converts poorly.
4. **Reposting others' renders / visible 3rd-party watermarks** — recommendation-ineligible (watermark deprioritization is ✅ Meta policy; the "10 reposts/30 days → excluded" threshold is 🟡).
5. **Engagement-bait, follow/unfollow churn, banned/broken hashtags** — reach restriction. ✅/🟡
6. **Single-image-only feed** — leaves cold-reach (Reels) and saves (carousels) on the table.

## 10. Caption templates (no product story needed)
> Across all: **line 1 = hook** (only ~125 chars show before "more"), **white space between lines**,
> **one specific SAVE/SHARE CTA** (the compounding metrics for art), hashtags last or in first comment,
> emoji as sparse anchors only (rotate/omit 🌊 — don't make one emoji mandatory). Use a genuine open
> question OR a save CTA, not both stacked, and skip "👇"-style pointer-bait.

- **T1 — Sensory hook + save CTA (default, short):**
  `Teal folding into gold, the moment before it spills over.` / `[the feeling it evokes]` / `Save this for a grey day. 🌊`
- **T2 — Question hook (drives comments):**
  `[one evocative line]` / `If this were a sound, what would it be?`
- **T3 — Process / story (occasional, medium; earns saves + follows):**
  `[what I was chasing]` / `I wanted color to feel like [motion/emotion] — no subject, just the phenomenon.` / `№ [N] in the [series] series. Follow as the palette evolves →`
- **T4 — Series / collection (gives a reason to follow):**
  `№[N]. [one sensory line]` / `Part of "[series]" — [the through-line].` / `Save the set. 🔖`
- **T5 — Pure minimalist (let the art lead):**
  `Color, mid-sentence.` 🌊
