# World-Views — candidate creative directions

Each is a cohesive aesthetic *universe* (not a single prompt): a recurring concept + subject domain +
mood + visual signature + the conceptual move it leans on. All branch from the blend of the two
references (`giz.akdag` crisp surreal-editorial × `marianopeccinetti` retro dreamy collage), each
shifted in a different direction. Pick **1–2** to build out — each becomes a dedicated moodboard +
prompt set + Instagram lane. See [[idea-bank]] for the conceptual moves, [[style-definition]] for the
blend, [[reference-accounts]] for the source analysis.

---

## 1. Still Poetry — *静かな詩* (Mariano-leaning)

- **One-liner**: a lone figure or object adrift in a vast, faded dreamscape — quiet, nostalgic, cosmic.
- **Subjects**: solitary figures, small houses, animals, water, sky, moons & planets.
- **Mood / finish**: faded retro film grain, dusty muted palette, soft light, contemplative.
- **Signature move**: mundane-on-cosmic-stage; tiny human vs vast nature.
- **Sample**: `a lone figure by a still lake at dusk, an enormous moon rising, faded film tone`

## 2. Bold Play — *大胆な遊び* (giz-leaning + the soccer interest)

- **One-liner**: dynamic figures in surreal sport and games — bold color, witty scale, motion.
- **Subjects**: athletes, soccer, running, leaping, balls; absurd/impossible sport.
- **Mood / finish**: saturated color-blocking, crisp daylight, energetic, playful.
- **Signature move**: mundane-on-cosmic-stage + scale-play.
- **Sample**: `a footballer mid-bicycle-kick against a giant setting sun, bold color, motion blur`

## 3. Editorial Mirage — *蜃気楼のエディトリアル* (fashion lane)

- **One-liner**: sculptural fashion figures in surreal painterly landscapes — quiet luxury meets dream.
- **Subjects**: models, avant-garde wardrobe, fabric & drape, surreal backdrops.
- **Mood / finish**: refined, painterly-photographic, controlled palette, soft editorial light.
- **Signature move**: object-as-monument (the garment is the hero) on a surreal stage.
- **Sample**: `full-body editorial, a model in flowing sculptural drapery on a bold monochrome plain, soft daylight`

## 4. Scale Theater — *スケールの劇場* (concept-driven)

- **One-liner**: a world of giants and tiny humans — wonder, fable, awe.
- **Subjects**: colossal animals / objects with tiny people; or giants over miniature worlds.
- **Mood / finish**: bold + dreamy, cinematic scale, sense of wonder.
- **Signature move**: scale inversion as the entire world.
- **Sample**: `a tiny person walking beneath a colossal sleeping animal the size of a hill, soft dawn light`

## 5. Domestic Cosmos — *日常の宇宙* (concept-driven)

- **One-liner**: ordinary domestic life staged in impossible, cosmic settings — wit and poetry.
- **Subjects**: furniture, everyday acts (reading, bathing, playing piano) in surreal/cosmic places.
- **Mood / finish**: warm, witty, surreal, lightly retro.
- **Signature move**: impossible juxtaposition + object-as-monument.
- **Sample**: `a person reading in an armchair that floats among the clouds at golden hour`

## Round 2 — the #1–#2 band, no soccer (2026-06-20)

User leans toward the surreal fine-art × figure × nature/cosmos zone (between Still Poetry and Bold
Play), without the soccer specificity. Five new candidates along a **quiet → bold** spectrum:

### 2a. Quiet Rituals — *静かな儀式* (most contemplative)
- A lone figure performing a small poetic act in a vast still landscape.
- Subjects: planting, carrying water, lighting a lantern, releasing birds, tending something.
- Mood: tender, narrative, faded warm light. Move: mundane act made monumental by the vast setting.
- `a lone figure releasing a flock of white birds across a vast misty valley at dawn`

### 2b. Elemental Companions — *元素の伴侶*
- Humans and animals in surreal, tender harmony within immense nature.
- Subjects: a person + a giant gentle animal; swimming with a whale; walking with deer in fog.
- Mood: mythic, gentle, dreamy. Move: scale-play + juxtaposition, but warm not absurd.
- `a child walking beside an enormous gentle elk through silver fog, soft light`

### 2c. Solitary Wonder — *孤独な畏怖* (awe; mid-spectrum)
- A single human facing a sublime, impossible natural spectacle.
- Subjects: a figure before a giant wave, an aurora, a colossal cloud, a falling star.
- Mood: awe, sublime, cinematic. Move: scale inversion (human tiny before the sublime).
- `a lone figure before a towering wave frozen in golden light, vast empty beach`

### 2d. Dreamt Weather — *夢の天気*
- Humans interacting with impossible sky and weather.
- Subjects: walking on clouds, under a rain of light/petals, touching celestial things.
- Mood: poetic, luminous, soft. Move: impossible juxtaposition (human × sky phenomenon).
- `a person walking across a field of floating clouds under a sky raining soft light`

### 2e. Chromatic Solitude — *色彩の孤独* (most bold)
- Vast bold color-field landscapes where a tiny figure is the only accent — color IS the subject.
- Subjects: enormous single-color fields / skies, one small figure, graphic minimal-surreal.
- Mood: striking, meditative, modern. Move: object-as-monument + solitude (color-blocking).
- `a tiny figure crossing an immense field of vivid red under a clean blue sky, bold color-blocking`
